Artist management & performance

I manage and represent performing artists who operate between tradition and contemporary expression. My current roster includes the Hazayana Collective (Tribal Fusion & Sacred Bass, France), Naomi Runa (Progressive Soul & Conscious Hip-Hop, Stockholm), Andreas Lång (Visblues & Swedish roots, Stockholm), Sophie Von Matern (Medicine Music & Ethno-Soul, Sweden), and Felicia Falck (Nordic Folk & Vispop, Bohuslän).

Before management, I spent a decade on the other side of the stage — DJing electronic and psychedelic music as Moonkin, performing fire shows, and founding Stockholm Flow, Scandinavia's largest flow arts community. I understand what artists need because I've needed it myself.
